Pathway Forward AI 0.9.3 - Khofi Guided Chat Experience

- Redesigns AI Service Navigator into a guided, one-question-at-a-time experience.
- Adds Khofi as the platform AI helper with reusable states:
  welcoming, listening, thinking/loading, guiding, celebrating,
  concerned/needs clarification, human-support handoff, error/retry.
- Adds accessible guided chat controls: Continue, Back, Start Over, Contact Support.
- Adds step progress indicator, structured response rendering, action cards, and optional Show more details.
- Preserves conversation persistence and support escalation with coordinator case creation.
- Keeps AI credentials server-side and preserves existing Mission Control and service pathway functionality.
